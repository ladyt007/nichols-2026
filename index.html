About Paul

Nearly Two Decades of
Fighting for Queens

Paul Nichols is an attorney, community leader, and veteran government leader with nearly two decades of experience working in and around government, law, and public policy. Throughout his career, he has dedicated himself to fighting for fairness, opportunity, and justice for everyday people.

Nichols previously ran for Governor of New York in 2022, bringing attention to the challenges facing working families across the state and advocating for stronger leadership to address the rising cost of living and unequal access to opportunity.

As a pastor and community leader in Queens, Nichols has spent years working directly with families, youth, and community organizations to strengthen neighborhoods and expand opportunities. Now he is running for the New York State Assembly to bring experienced leadership and real solutions to Albany.

Platform

Real Solutions for
District 32

The challenges facing Queens are real — but they are not unsolvable. With the right leadership and priorities, there is an answer.

01
🏘️
Lower Cost of Living

Queens families are facing an affordability crisis. Paul Nichols is committed to advancing policies that lower the cost of living, protect working families, support small businesses, and ensure that people can afford to live and thrive in the communities they call home.

02
🏫
Fair Resources for Our Communities

Every community deserves fair access to resources and opportunity. Paul Nichols will fight for equity in health care, education, infrastructure, and essential services so that every neighborhood in District 32 receives the support it needs.

03
🏙️
Stronger Neighborhoods

Strong communities are built through investment, opportunity, and stability. Paul Nichols supports policies that strengthen families, expand opportunity for young people, and ensure that neighborhoods remain safe, vibrant, and resilient.

04
⚕️
Healthcare Access

Expanding access to affordable healthcare and mental health services, and protecting the community clinics that serve working families across District 32.

05
📚
Quality Education

Investing in our public schools, supporting teachers, improving resources for students, and expanding after-school and youth programs for every child in the district.

06

Smart, community-based public safety solutions that protect families and neighborhoods while building trust between residents and institutions.
